For this performance you will need a stable connection to the internet and the ability to submit text input.

You do not need a camera or microphone and will not be seen or heard on screen.

The purpose of Litmus is to create a space for genuine connection between performers and audiences in a historical period defined by isolation and tumult.

Each iteration of Litmus accommodates 1 participant via this portal, however each iteration is livestreamed to our YouTube Live account to increase access to the performance and to raise money to support accessible arts programming in southern California.

Litmus asks that patrons create an account via Eventbrite in order to access the performance. This account is subject to Eventbrite’s terms and conditions.

Patrons are also asked to share some personal information during the performance. In doing so, patrons make a connection with the performer and invite them to create a unique performance for you based on your input. This information will only be seen by the performer and will not be shared on the YouTube Live stream or with any third party. The performer will make reference to this input without sharing any personal or private information. During the performance, participants are asked to share some personal information. Litmus Uses Cookies. Many sites use cookies and/or other technologies to place information about a user’s visit to that website in a small data text file that is stored on the user’s computer. Litmus does use “cookies” or similar technologies in connection with this website in order to facilitate the user experience.

How Litmus Uses Information Collected Through This Site:

  • Litmus is an experience dedicated to connection and does not sell or share user data with any 3rd parties or advertisers.
  • Litmus uses user submitted data to construct a unique dance performance for each patron/participant.
  • User submitted input will be seen by the performer. Performers will reference this input without sharing any private details of this input.

Children Under the Age of Thirteen. Litmus does not knowingly collect data or personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not provide any personal information on or to this Site. If you believe that a child under 13 has submitted personal information on or through this Site without the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian, please contact Litmus so that appropriate action can be taken.
